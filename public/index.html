SLVR Auto-Staker

Welcome to your auto-staker

This app claims the staking rewards your locked SLVR earns and reinvests them automatically, the way you choose. It runs only on this computer and starts in dry-run — it shows what it would do and sends nothing — until you decide to go live.

It uses real money on a real chain. We'll walk through each part before anything can spend a cent, and you'll see a preview of the very first live action before it happens.

How it earns — three ways

Worth understanding before any money moves. Each behaves differently.

💧 Staking rewards
Your locked SLVR continuously earns ETH. The app claims it for you and splits each claim.
⛏️ Mining
Grid bets that are statistically favorable over time but individually risky — any single round can lose. It's betting.
🔒 Buy & lock (compounding)
Buys SLVR and max-locks it into your position. This is permanent — locked SLVR is burned and can never be withdrawn.
